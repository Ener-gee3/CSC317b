Navigating COVID-19

At Kleinfelder, the health and safety of our employees is a primary core value, and this value also extends to clients, subcontractors, the general public, and other parties in proximity to where we are working. Kleinfelder has proactively instituted processes and procedures to address the risks associated with COVID-19. We continue to actively monitor the evolving COVID-19 situation, including guidance provided by the CDC, Public Health Agency of Canada, WHO, and government and local agencies.
